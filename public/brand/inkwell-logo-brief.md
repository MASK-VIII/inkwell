1. Inkwell is a **local-first writing workspace**: the mark should feel calm, durable, and trustworthy—not flashy or startup-generic.
2. Use **at most two flat colors** per lockup (no gradients, no hairline engraving); shapes only, readable as a **silhouette at 24px**.
3. **No strokes thinner than ~2px** at 32×32 (avoid feather barbs, cross-hatching, or ornamental serifs in the icon).
4. Palette stays in the **warm ink + parchment** family already used in the app (deep brown ground, cream paper accents).
5. Prefer a **single bold idea** (well depth, open book, or ink point)—if it needs a caption to parse, it is too complex.
